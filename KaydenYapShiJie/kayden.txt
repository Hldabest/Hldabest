Kayden Yap Shi Jie, age 12, began his piano studies at the age of six. He is currently a student
of the Gifted Young Pianists course at NAFA School of Young Talents under the tutelage of Ms
Lena Ching. He has obtained his ABRSM Grade 8 practical with distinction in March 2017 and
is currently pursuing his Diploma studies. Kayden was a Gold Prize winner in Junior B category
at the Singapore-Asia Piano Competition 2017 and awarded an opportunity to compete at the
22nd Japan PIARA Competition at Hamamatsu in July 2018. He also won a 2ndPrize in the
Junior category of the 1st Nanyang International Music Competition 2017.
